Few words about us

Since we opened our first location in Manhattan’s West Village in 2015, Bakerlab has become world-famous for its classic American desserts and baked goods. Over the years, many of our creations have become iconic, including buttercream-crowned cupcakes, elegant red velvet and hummingbird cakes. Whether you’re visiting our original corner bakery or one of the many Bakerlab locations around the world, you’ll always be welcomed into a warm, inviting space, where each treat we serve is made the Bakerlab way: from scratch, by hand, and with love.
